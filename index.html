How to make Chicken Sandwich

Image by timolina on Freepik

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bread Roll
  • 1 Chicken Cutlet
  • American Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Honey mustard
  • Hot sauce
  • White sauce

Directions

  1. Turn on stove to high heat and put vegatable oil
  2. Place chicken cutlet on stove for 3-4 minutes
  3. Put mayonaise on each side of bread roll
  4. Put all sauces listed in ingrediants on bread
  5. Place tomatoes on top and then lettuce
  6. Flip the chicken cutlet and leave it for 1-2 minutes
  7. Cut up the chicken cutlet into smaller pieces and leave it for a minute
  8. Place cheese on top and then move it onto the bread
  9. Put salt and pepper onto the sandwich then close it

*Optional*

If you want to toast the bread you can place the bread into a counter top oven until crispy
